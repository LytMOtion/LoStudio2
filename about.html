02  The Bio

Fifteen years on the Central Coast.

For more than fifteen years, Daniel Harper has guided buyers and investors across the Central Coast through financing built around their goals — explained in plain language, with the details handled before they become problems.

As Senior Mortgage Advisor at Coastal Bridge Home Loans, Daniel works with first-time buyers, growing families, seasoned investors, and self-employed borrowers whose income deserves a closer read.

When a loan is the right move, he structures it precisely. When it isn't, he says so — which is why so much of his business comes from past clients and the agents who refer them.
